Cryptographic Signing Key
Your signing key is a unique cryptographic identity tied to your account. When you e-sign a document, this key proves it was you — and it's verifiable in the audit trail.

Keys are deterministically derived from your identity and cannot be transferred.
⏰ You can regenerate your key in 24 hours.

How It Works
1.Generate your key — it's derived from your identity using HMAC-SHA256 + timestamp salt.
2.When you sign a document, you enter a PIN. Your signature is: SHA256(username:role:pin:docId)
3.The signature is stored in the audit trail and can be independently verified.
⚠ REGENERATION POLICY
You may regenerate your signing key once every 24 hours. Regenerating invalidates all previous unsigned documents.
